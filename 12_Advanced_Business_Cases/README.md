# Advanced Business Cases

## Objective

Solve real business problems using advanced SQL queries and techniques.

## Business Questions

- Which product generates the highest revenue in each category?
- How can customers be ranked based on total revenue?
- How does revenue change from one month to the next?
- What is the cumulative (running) revenue over time?
- Who are the top 10% of customers based on revenue?
- Which product generates the highest revenue in each market?

## SQL Concepts Used

- Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
- Window Functions
- ROW_NUMBER()
- RANK()
- NTILE()
- LAG()
- SUM() OVER()
- PARTITION BY
- Aggregate Functions (SUM())
- JOIN
- GROUP BY
- NULLIF()
- ROUND()

## Files Included

- 12_Advanced_Business_Cases.sql

## Business Impact

These queries answer more complex business questions that cannot be solved using simple SQL. 
They help identify top-performing products and customers, analyze business growth over time and compare performance across different markets.
