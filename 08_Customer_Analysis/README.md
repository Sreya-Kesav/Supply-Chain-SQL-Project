# Customer Analysis

## Objective

Analyze customer purchasing pattern and identify valuable customers.

## Business Questions

- Who are the top customers by revenue?
- Who are the top customers by profit?
- Which customers place the most orders?
- What is the average order value (AOV) for each customer?
- Which customers spend more than the average customer?

## SQL Concepts Used

- JOIN
- GROUP BY
- CTE
- Aggregate Functions
- Views

## Files Included

- 08_Customer_Analysis.sql
- 08A_Customer_Analysis_Using_Views.sql

## Business Impact

This analysis helps identify valuable customers and understand how customers spend. It can help with retaining customers and marketing decisions.
